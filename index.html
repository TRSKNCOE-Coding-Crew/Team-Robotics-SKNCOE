Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our team

How can I join the team?

We typically hold recruitment drives at the beginning of each academic year. Keep an eye on our social media channels for announcements. We look for passionate students from all engineering disciplines.

What skills do I need to join?

While experience in programming (Python, C++), CAD software, or electronics is a plus, the most important thing is a willingness to learn and a passion for robotics. We provide training and mentorship to all new members.

What kind of projects do you work on?

We work on a wide range of projects, from autonomous robots for competitions to research-oriented projects in areas like computer vision and AI. You can see some of our past projects in the "Projects" section of our website.
